Contract 🔒


0x218DcDc9B696FeA88cDEE7b68609298e1b2aA199

This contract is for a cryptocurrency called OuchCoin and listed below are some of it's key points.

1. **Transaction Fees**: Every time someone sends OuchCoin to someone else, a small part (1%) of the coins being sent is destroyed (burned). This is called a "burn tax".

2. **Penalties for Inactivity**: If someone holds onto OuchCoin without using it for over a year, they're charged a penalty when they do eventually use it. If they haven't done anything with their coins for over a year and have more than 100 coins, they have to transfer all of their coins at once. This means they'll pay the penalty on all the coins they have, not just the ones they're currently sending. The penalty starts at 5% per year of inactivity but can't go higher than 25%.

4. **Events**: Whenever coins are burned the contract records it in an event, so anyone can see what's happening.

5. **Supply and Name**: The initial supply of OuchCoin is 420.69 trillion coins, and the symbol for the coin is "OUCH".

6. **Disclaimer**: The contract emphasizes that OuchCoin is just for fun and shouldn't be treated like a serious investment. It's meant for entertainment purposes only, and people should be careful with their money.
